Vault Documentation

Centralize secret management, rotate old credentials, generate credentials on demand, audit client interactions, and support regulatory compliance.

  • CLI
  • APIs

Why use Vault?

  • Manage static secrets
    Store, rotate, and encrypt arbitrary strings as key-value pairs
  • Manage certificates
    Integrate with certificate authorities like KMIP and PKI to manage certificate life cycles and authenticate clients
  • Manage identities and authentication
    Control client access to sensitive information with managed entities, identity tokens, OIDC workflows, and workload identity federation (WIF)
  • Manage 3rd-party secrets
    Generate and revoke on-demand credentials and control access to external encryption keys and cloud credentials
  • Manage sensitive data
    Encrypt or tokenize sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Support regulatory compliance
    Use Vault as part of an HSM solution, FIPS compliant architecture, or PKCS11 authN workflow
